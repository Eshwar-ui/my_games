Privacy Policy for My Games

Effective date: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the My Games app (com.eshwar.my_games).

Overview

My Games is a simple offline games app. Based on the current version of the app, it does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal or sensitive user data.

Information We Collect

The app stores limited gameplay information locally on your device, such as:

  • high scores and similar game progress values saved with local app storage;
  • temporary in-memory gameplay state while you are using the app.

This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to the developer or to third parties by the app.

How We Use Information

Local gameplay data is used only to:

  • save high scores;
  • preserve basic game-related preferences or progress on the device;
  • support normal gameplay features.

Data Sharing

My Games does not share user data with third parties.

The app does not include account creation, cloud sync, analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or in-app purchases in the current version of this repository.

Data Retention and Deletion

Any locally stored gameplay data remains on your device until one of the following happens:

  • you clear the app's storage through your device settings;
  • you uninstall the app;
  • a game feature allows you to reset or overwrite saved scores.

Because the app does not collect or store your personal data on external servers, there is no server-side account or cloud profile to delete in the current version.

Security

The app relies on the storage protections provided by the Android operating system and the app sandbox. No method of storage is guaranteed to be perfectly secure, but the current version of the app is designed to keep gameplay data on the device only.

Children's Privacy

My Games does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Third-Party Services

The app itself does not send your data to third-party services in the current version.

If you download the app through Google Play or use Android system services, Google or your device manufacturer may process certain technical information under their own terms and privacy policies. That processing is outside the app developer's direct control.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app's features or data practices change. If future versions add features such as analytics, ads, cloud saves, sign-in, or online services, this Privacy Policy should be updated before those features are released.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the app's data practices, use the developer contact information listed on the Google Play store listing for My Games.
